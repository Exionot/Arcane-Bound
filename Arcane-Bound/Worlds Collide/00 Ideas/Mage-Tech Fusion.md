# How it works 
Specialized electronic boards and chips are made in order to utilize the energy coming from the enchantments of [[Arcaenian Magic]]. These electronics chips are used to amplify and precisely control the power of spells. Objects equipped with these chips are able to use [[Arcaenian Magic]] without an [[Arcaenian Band]]. It is also capable of replicating spells from different [[Arcaenian Band|Arcaenian Bands]].
