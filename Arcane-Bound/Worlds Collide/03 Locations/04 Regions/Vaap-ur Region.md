# **Description** 
**Vaap-ur** is a region found in the shores of the [[Northern Lands]]. It is home to [[Vaapuron]]—a rare amphibious creature of the sea. ^beee8b

[[Shellport Capital]], capital city of the [[Aquarin Kingdom]], is located in **Vaap-ur Region**, along with [[Fontur City]]—a city of [[Elemental Magic##Hyro|Hydro Magic]].
