# **Description** 
**Split Region** is one of the core regions of [[NeoTech Union]]. The [[Hydro River]] flows through the region splitting it into two subregions, **NTU-02A** and **NTU-02B**. The nation's capital, [[Fluxpoint City]], is located on both sides of the river, bridging the two subregions together. ^f4a759
