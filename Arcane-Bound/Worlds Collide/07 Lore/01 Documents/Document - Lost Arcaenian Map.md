# Overview
The **Lost Arcaenian Map** is believed to be an ancient cartographic record of [[Arcaena]]'s territorial division system. It illustrates how ancient lands were divided highlighting a structured hierarchy that ancient civilization followed and is still applies in the present day.

# Division of Locations
Continents are divided into **Lands**, Kingdoms are divided into **Regions**, and large settlements in regions are divided into **Quarters**.

**Format**: `[Continent]` -> `[Land]` -> `[Kingdom]` -> `[Region]` -> `[Settlement]` -> `[Quarter]` -> `[Point of Interest]`

# Interpretation of Findings
| Layer             | Interpretation                                                   |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Continent         | A large landmass within the planet                               |
| Land              | Large divisions within a continent                                     |
| Kingdom           | A large political settlement, governing multiple regions |
| Region            | A division within a kingdom                                      |
| Settlement        | Includes cities, towns, and villages                             |
| Quarter           | A division of a settlement                                       |
| Point of Interest | A specific location                                             |
