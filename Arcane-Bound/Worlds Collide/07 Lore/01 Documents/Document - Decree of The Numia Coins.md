# Overview 
In the name and power of **King Fulgor Ignia II, King of Ignia**, the kingdom hereby declares the **Numia Coins** to become the standard for all lawful trades across the lands of [[Arcaena]].

# Authorized Coinage 

| Material | Insignia                          | Value                  |
| -------- | --------------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Gold     | Royal Symbol of The Ignia Kingdom | 1 Gold Numia Coin      |
| Silver   | Crown of King Ignia II            | 1/10 Gold Numia Coins  |
| Bronze   | Sword of King Ignia II            | 1/100 Gold Numia Coins |

^2422da

## Legal Provisions 
- All trades, debts, and taxes across the kingdoms of [[Arcaena]] shall use the coinage above. ^7c8182
- Creation of counterfeits shall be punished with death. ^ddc145

# Enactment 
This decree is impressed with the **Royal Seal of Ignia**, witnessed by the [[High Council of Flamma]].

By my name and power,
**King Fulgor Ignia II**
*King, and ruler of Ignia*
