**Document Classification**: Public Registry

## Overview
To ensure consistency across all digital and physical infrastructure, the HyperCorp Republic enforces a universal designation system for sectors, zones, and administrative regions. This codex outlines all naming conventions used in Solid-Light Energy urban grid layouts, transport sectors, and defense-zoned infrastructure.

---

## Regional Labeling Format
- `FX-00` → Fluxpoint City, Sector 00  
- `PF-01` → PhotonForge City, Sector 01  
- `LS-05` → Lightspeed City, Sector 05  

**Format**: `[City Code]-[Sector Number]`
