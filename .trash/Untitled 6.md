# Republic of Technosia - Official Currency Specification

## Currency Name
**Technosian Credit (TCR)**

## Issuing Authority
**Technosia Central Bank (TCB)**  
Website: [www.tcb-technosia.gov](http://www.tcb-technosia.gov)

## Legal Status
Technosian Credit (TCR) is the **only legal tender** within the Republic of Technosia, as per the **Monetary Policy Act of 2085**.

## Security Features (Banknotes)
- Holographic strip
- Transparent window
- Color-shifting ink
- Microtext
- UV-reactive fibers

## Currency Symbol
**₮** (Unicode: U+20AE)

## Exchange Rate Policy
The TCR is a **free-floating currency**. Exchange rates are determined by market forces but monitored by TCB to prevent excessive volatility.

## Digital Currency (TCR-D)
A digital version of the TCR is available:
- Blockchain-based
- Centralized validator nodes operated by TCB
- Digital wallets issued via verified financial institutions

### TCR-D Technical Specifications
- **Protocol**: TCRChain v1.2
- **Block Time**: 5 seconds
- **Consensus**: Proof-of-Authority (PoA)
- **Max Supply**: Dynamic, adjusted by TCB policy

## Notes
- Defacing, counterfeiting, or unauthorized reproduction of TCR is a criminal offense.
- Annual inflation target: **2% ± 0.5%**
